About Me

I am an instructional designer and researcher passionate about using generative AI to create inclusive and accessible learning experiences for global audiences.

With a multidimensional background spanning academic research, EdTech product development, and global non-profits across the U.S. and China, I specialize in bridging pedagogical theory with emerging technology. My experience ranges from optimizing MOOC operations and K-12 products to designing AI-integrated curricula for adult learners. I combine rigorous user research with data-driven instructional design to build scalable, inclusive learning ecosystems that drive engagement and performance.

I believe AI is not a replacement for human connection, but a bridge to overcome resource barriers and dismantle educational inequality. I strive to leverage technology to democratize access to high-quality learning for all.
